# Content Repurpose Skill

## Inputs

- Source artifact (thread, note, research summary)
- Core thesis
- CTA target

## Produce

- X draft (short, punchy)
- LinkedIn draft (structured insight)
- Reddit draft (discussion-first)
- Optional newsletter excerpt

## Guardrails

- Keep core claim consistent across channels.
- Adapt tone and format, not facts.
- Remove fluff; maximize specific takeaways.
